2. Leonardo Williams
Business Development
Mayor Leonardo (Leo) Williams is a dedicated public servant, entrepreneur, and former educator committed to the unity and prosperity of Durham. A two-time Durham Public Schools Teacher of the Year and proud graduate of North Carolina Central University, Leo transitioned from school administration to small business ownership when he and his wife, Zweli, opened the first authentic Zimbabwean restaurant in the United States. His innovative leadership has been recognized by the Triangle Business Journal’s “40 Under 40” and even touted by President Joe Biden as the "American Story."

Elected to the City Council in 2021 and sworn in as Mayor in 2023, Leo leverages his background in education and entrepreneurship to address social issues and enhance public safety. Beyond his local impact, he supports his family’s international business, TICOZ Enterprises, one of Zimbabwe’s largest textile factories. Guided by his roles as a husband and father, Mayor Williams continues to apply his strategic vision and passion for service to ensure Durham remains a vibrant community for all.
